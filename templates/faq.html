Gracy World Initiative is a social impact organization dedicated to empowering the next generation by bridging the gap between technology and emotional intelligence. Founded by Oreoluwa Grace Filani, GWI focuses on providing young scholars with high-demand AI productivity skills while building mental health resilience to help them navigate and reform modern systems.

Our programs are primarily designed for students and young scholars who are eager to enhance their digital literacy and personal development. While some events are tailored to specific regions, such as our recent focus on Ikorodu, Lagos, we aim to provide resources and training that inspire youth from all backgrounds to dream beyond their immediate circumstances.

We believe that technical skills alone are not enough for long-term success. GWI adopts a holistic approach where AI productivity training is paired with mental health support and resilience workshops. This ensures our participants are not only tech-savvy but also emotionally equipped to handle the pressures of the digital age and professional environments.

Inquiries submitted through the contact page are stored in the database and shown on the admin dashboard for review.

Project 1,000 is a flagship seminar series aimed at transforming the lives of 1,000 students. The primary objectives are to:

  • a. Equip students with practical AI productivity tools.
  • b. Foster mental health awareness and emotional intelligence.
  • c. Rewrite local narratives of vulnerability into blueprints for tech innovation and global visibility.

GWI is deeply committed to global progress, specifically targeting SDG 4 (Quality Education) by providing accessible tech training and SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals) by collaborating with strategic partners to drive impactful social change and sustainable community development.

We are always looking for strategic partners, donors, and volunteers who share our vision for youth empowerment. You can support us by:

  • Mentorship: Sharing your expertise in AI or mental health.
  • Sponsorship: Funding training materials and seminar logistics.
  • Collaboration: Partnering with us for future Project series to expand our reach to more communities.

Please reach out via our contact page for more details on how to get involved.
